Download It

How-to?

Download videos from almost anywhere and everywhere over the Internet using our completely free Download It.


There are a few exceptions though, for example, you wont be able to download videos if:

  • You entered a wrong url.
  • You are trying to download video from any of our unsupported sites. For ex. Daily Motion
  • You entered a url video that needs user authentication. For ex. Instagram's Private Account's posts
  • You are trying to download it from any webpage which is banned in your country(For ex. TikTok in India)

And finally if the given URL is not amoung the above given exceptions you would be able to download your wanted video.


There are a large amount of website supported with us, some of them are:

  • YouTube.com
  • Instagram.com
  • Twitter.com
  • Reddit.com
  • TikTok.com etc.

Steps

Downloading videos from any site has only three steps:

  1. Getting the URL of the video.
  2. Pasting it in the URL bar of Download-It and click Download
  3. Then in the video playback click on the menu (three dots) and then click Download

And just with these simple steps you can download any video that you want!


Here are a few examples to download videos from some of these sites.
